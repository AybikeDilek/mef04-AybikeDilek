Korean Triage Analysis¶

Aybike Dilek¶

Date: 30.01.2021¶

The project aims to conduct exploratory analyses and to provide outcomes using the Korean Triage dataset.

About Dataset

The dataset contains various data points of patients who visited emergency departments between 2016-10 to 2017-09. There are 24 variables, with 1267 observations.

Overview

The dataset and packages were uploaded. The first five rows and data types of the columns were investigated. Some columns contained missing values like NRS_pain. Besides, the data types of some columns were not the desired type.

NRS-Pain-Classes were created manually. Class-0 and Class-1 contained respectively 1-4, 5-10 pain scores. The empty rows were removed. Under-triage (2) was changed as 1 for the Mistriage-Proportion calculation.

KTS-Duration did not change significantly with-in classes. Regional ED (2) had a lower KTS-Duration than Local ED (1) in both classes. Class-1 had a higher KTAS-Expert-Level in both groups. Length-of-Stay-Minutes deviated in both groups and classes. Mistriage-Proportion of Class-1 in Regional ED was ~%17 more than other combinations.

Since Regional ED is advance, patients, who were seriously ill, may be preferred. Therefore, the Length-of-Stay-Min of Regional ED may be higher.

Class-1 cases may be more difficult short KTS-Duration could be a disadvantage in Mistriage-Proportion.
